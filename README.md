# Women Representation in the Audiovisual Sector in France

## 1. Project Overview

This project is part of a Master's study unit ("Projet d’étude").
Its objective is to analyze the representation of women in the audiovisual sector in France, based on institutional data, statistical reports, and research studies.

The work focuses on identifying gender inequalities in media visibility, speech time, roles, and participation in production.

---

## 2. Objectives

* Measure the presence of women in audiovisual media (television, radio, cinema)
* Analyze the evolution of this representation over time
* Identify disparities between visibility and actual influence (e.g., speaking time)
* Study the role of women in content production (directors, authors, technical roles)
* Highlight structural inequalities using quantitative data

---

## 3. Data Sources

The analysis relies on official and reliable sources, including:

* ARCOM (Autorité de régulation de la communication audiovisuelle et numérique)
* CNC (Centre national du cinéma et de l’image animée)
* French Ministry of Culture
* INA (Institut national de l’audiovisuel)
* European Audiovisual Observatory
* UNESCO reports

The raw datasets are stored in the `DATA/` directory.

---

## 4. Methodology

The project follows a data-driven approach:

1. Data collection from institutional reports and datasets
2. Data cleaning and preprocessing (CSV, Excel, PDF extraction)
3. Data structuring and integration into a database
4. Statistical analysis
5. Visualization and interpretation of results
